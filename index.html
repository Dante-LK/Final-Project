Your Guide to the Star Wars Galaxy

Chronological Order

StarWars:The fhantom Menace

is a 1999 American epic space opera film written and directed by George Lucas

StarWars:Attack Of The Clones

is a 2002 American epic space opera film directed by George Lucas

StarWars:Clone Wars

is set predominantly between the events of Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones (2002) and Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith (2005).

StarWars:Revenge Of The Sith

is a 2005 American epic space opera film that is the sequel to The Phantom Menace (1999) and Attack of the Clones (2002).

StarWars:Solo

Solo: A Star Wars Story is a 2018 American space Western film[15][16] centering on the Star Wars character Han Solo. Directed by Ron Howard,

StarWars:Obi-Wan KENOBI

is an American space opera television miniseries produced by Lucasfilm for the streaming service Disney+. It is part of the Star Wars franchise.

StarWars:Rebels

is an American 3D animated science fiction television series produced by Lucasfilm Animation and set in the Star Wars universe.

StarWars:Andor

is an American science fiction television series created by Tony Gilroy for the streaming service Disney+. It is part of the Star Wars franchise,

StarWars:ROGUE ONE

is a 2016 American epic space opera film directed by Gareth Edwards. The screenplay was written by Chris Weitz and Tony Gilroy

StarWars:A New Hope

is a 1977 American epic space opera film written and directed by George Lucas,

StarWars:EMPIRE STRIKES BACK

is a 1980 American epic space opera film directed by Irvin Kershner based on a story by George Lucas.

StarWars:Return Of The Jedi

is a 1983 American epic space opera film that is a sequel to The Empire Strikes Back (1980).

StarWars:The MANDALORIAN

is an American space Western television series created by Jon Favreau for the streaming service Disney+.

StarWars:BOBA FETT

It is part of the Star Wars franchise and a spin-off from The Mandalorian.

StarWars:AHSOKA

Ahsoka follows former Jedi apprentice Ahsoka Tano and her allies as they fight the remnants of the Galactic Empire during the time of the fledgling New Republic.

StarWars:The Force Awakens

The sequel to Return of the Jedi (1983), it is chronologically the seventh film in the "Skywalker Saga". Set thirty years after Return of the Jedi.

StarWars:The Last Jedi

The Last Jedi follows Rey as she seeks the aid of Luke Skywalker in hopes of turning the tide for the Resistance in the fight against Kylo Ren and the First Order.

StarWars:The Rise Of Skywalker

Set after The Last Jedi, The Rise of Skywalker follows Rey, Finn, and Poe Dameron as they lead the remnants of the Resistance
